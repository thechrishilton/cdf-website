What We Do

Practical, affordable cybersecurity services designed for organizations like yours.

At CyberDefense Firm, we provide practical, affordable cybersecurity services to under-resourced organizations — especially small private schools, churches, and local businesses. Every service is designed to be understandable, actionable, and tailored to your needs.

Risk Assessments

We evaluate your current systems using industry-recognized frameworks like CIS Controls. You'll receive a clear, jargon-free PDF report that highlights your biggest risks and practical steps to fix them — even if you have no dedicated IT staff.

Request an Assessment

Staff Cybersecurity Training

We create simple, visual training materials to help your team spot phishing scams, use secure passwords, and stay safe online. Materials can be delivered via PDF handouts, Zoom sessions, or self-paced video — whatever works for your group.

Schedule Training

Incident Response Plans

Every organization needs a "what if" plan. We'll help you build one — a written protocol you can follow in the event of a cyberattack, email compromise, or data loss. It's your emergency game plan, simplified.

Develop Your Plan

Basic Compliance Support

Whether you need help understanding HIPAA, FERPA, or basic NIST Cybersecurity Framework principles, we'll simplify compliance checklists and help you meet the most important security requirements — without overwhelming you.

Get Compliance Help
