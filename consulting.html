Pro Consulting

Enterprise-grade cybersecurity. Community-level pricing.

CyberDefense Consulting LLC

Here's the deal: CyberDefense Firm is a nonprofit serving schools, churches, and nonprofits with free or low-cost security help. That's our core mission. But to scale that work, we started CyberDefense Consulting LLC — the for-profit side of the operation.

When we take on commercial clients at competitive rates, every dollar of profit flows directly back into the nonprofit's mission. You get enterprise-level cybersecurity expertise from the same team. The nonprofit gets sustainable funding. Your business stays secure. Everybody wins.

This is how we build a self-sustaining model for protecting underserved communities.

What We Offer

Enterprise Services, Real Pricing

Four core services. All delivered by the same team with government and private sector expertise.

Penetration Testing & Vulnerability Scans

We attack your systems the way real hackers would. Comprehensive scans plus hands-on testing to find exploitable weaknesses before criminals do. Detailed report with remediation priorities.

Compliance Audits

HIPAA, PCI-DSS, SOC 2, ISO 27001 — we map your systems against the standards you need to meet. Gap analysis, implementation roadmap, and documentation to prove compliance.

Security Architecture & Infrastructure Review

From cloud migrations to on-prem buildouts, we design security into your infrastructure. Network segmentation, access controls, data protection, and disaster recovery — built right the first time.

Managed Security Services

Don't have the budget for a full SOC? We monitor your infrastructure 24/7, detect threats in real-time, respond to incidents, and keep your security posture sharp with ongoing updates and tuning.
