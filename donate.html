Volunteer With Us

CyberDefense Firm relies on volunteers who are passionate about protecting underserved communities. Whether you're a cybersecurity professional, educator, or community advocate, there's a place for you on our team.

Volunteer Opportunities

  • Cybersecurity Professionals: Conduct assessments, lead training workshops, and mentor community organizations
  • Educators: Help develop and deliver cybersecurity awareness materials for schools and churches
  • Community Outreach: Raise awareness about our services and connect with local organizations
