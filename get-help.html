Who Qualifies?

We provide free cybersecurity support to qualifying organizations across the United States. Here's who we serve:

Schools & Educational Institutions

Public and private K-12 schools, charter schools, and educational nonprofits. We help protect student data, staff systems, and critical learning infrastructure.

Churches & Houses of Worship

Faith-based organizations serving your community. We protect donor information, member records, and facility systems while respecting your mission.

Small Businesses

Local businesses with limited IT resources. We help you establish baseline security, protect customer data, and keep operations secure without breaking the budget.

Veteran-Owned Organizations

Businesses and nonprofits founded by veterans. We're committed to serving those who've served, with practical security solutions tailored to your needs.
